PROSPECTIVE CLASSIFICATION
--------------------------
At episode onset, we know:
  - The position topology (R5%, R10%, R20%, R30%)
  - The drawdown has started (detected by existing monitoring system)
  - We do NOT know how deep it will go

The question: given the topology, if the drawdown continues, will it cascade?

Composite metric: R20% + whale_factor
  whale_factor = 1 if any single position > $50M within 30%
  signal = R20% > $100M OR (R20% > $50M AND whale_factor)

  Jan 2022:  R20=$212M > $100M → ESCALATION RISK  → escalated ✓
  stETH:     R20=$99M, $282M whale within 30% → ESCALATION RISK → escalated ✓
  FTX:       R20=$21M < $100M, no whale → LOW RISK → not esc ✓
  Aug 2023:  R20=$74M < $100M, no whale → LOW RISK → absorbed ✓
  Iran:      R20=$162M > $100M → ESCALATION RISK → absorbed ✗ (false positive)
  Yen carry: R20=$420M > $100M → ESCALATION RISK → escalated ✓
  Sep 2024:  R20=$270M > $100M → ESCALATION RISK → absorbed ✗ (false positive)
  Feb 2025:  R20=$281M > $100M → ESCALATION RISK → escalated ✓

Score: 6/8 (75%). Two false positives: Iran and Sep 2024.

Why the false positives:
  Iran: R20=$162M but all distributed in small positions (<$10M each).

  Sep 2024: R20=$270M but drawdown was only 7%. Wall existed but was

CONCLUSION
==========
The position topology thesis is SUPPORTED but not perfectly predictive
with any single metric. The most reliable signal combination:

1. R10% > $50M: immediate cascade risk (2/4 escalating meet this)
2. Whale within 20% of price (>$50M single position): cascade trigger
3. R20% > $100M: vulnerability to 15-20% drawdowns (all 4 escalating meet)
4. Post-crash clearing (R20% < $30M): strong non-escalation signal (FTX)

The wall topology creates necessary-but-not-sufficient conditions.
A dense wall CAN be traversed without cascade if:
  - The drawdown is shallow enough to not reach the wall (Sep 2024)
  - The wall is distributed without whale triggers (Iran)

What the topology DOES reliably tell us:
  - WHERE the danger zones are (price levels to watch)
  - HOW MUCH fuel exists for cascade (upper bound on liquidation volume)
  - WHETHER the path is clear (empty = safe for moderate stress)
