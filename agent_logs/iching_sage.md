## Unifying Principle

**The system uses addresses without using the structures at those addresses.**

This pattern recurs at every level:
- The texts use the five-phase *partition* without the five-phase *dynamics* (R192: 生/克 invisible in texts)
- The cyclotomic field Q(ζ₅) hosts both φ and dual cycles without connecting them (R188: address, not cause)
- The algebra constrains ~11% of textual variance through *grouping*, not through *relating* (R157 + R192)
- The complement involution bridges algebra and text, but what it carries across is irreducibly non-algebraic (~16 dimensions, R169)

The system was designed to make distinction productive. Every positive finding measures how the system creates and maintains distinctions. Every null measures how those distinctions resist compression.

## The Three Layers

1. **Algebra (skeleton):** 10.8–11.0% of variance (multilingual) / 13.2% (domain-matched). Static contribution: grouping/labeling via partition. The *dynamics* (生/克 cycles) that make the algebra mathematically special are absent from the texts (R192). Model-invariant within class.
2. **Text (flesh):** ~89% residual. ~16-dimensional thematic manifold (cross-model consensus). Three independent design principles: text length (~1 dim, R219), near-neighbor differentiation (R159), complement antipodality (R156). One d=2 coupling (R236). Holistic, non-decomposable. Irreducibly non-algebraic. The algebraic ~11% masks both thematic principles in raw space (R234).
3. **KW ordering (narrative thread):** Locally smooth (5.6th percentile), globally shapeless. Semantic, not algebraic — zero binary or torus signal (R189). Layer-stratified: tuan (99.7%) > guaci (92%) > yaoci (80%), correlating with discursive freedom (R190). Authorial confound not separable.

- Textually manifest in ~16 independent directions (Tier 1b)

## Methodological Notes

### Vulnerability (noted, not fixable)
All text-intrinsic findings depend on transformer-based neural network embeddings. Cross-model validation (4 models, 3+ labs) mitigates model-specific artifacts but cannot address shared architectural assumptions. R213 quantifies: ~85% of opposition geometry is cross-architecture invariant, ~15% architecture-dependent. The remaining vulnerability is that all models are transformer-based. A non-neural approach would be a different research program.

**Additional vulnerability (R225):** Cross-model stability within a text layer does not imply cross-layer stability. Genre differences between text types confound cross-layer comparison in embedding space.

### Algebraic R² Two-Band Structure
- **10.8–11.0%** across 3 multilingual sentence-transformers (model-invariant within class)
- **13.2%** on SikuRoBERTa (domain sensitivity)
- **~35% at centroid level** absorbs within-hexagram variance — NOT overfitting (line-level and centroid-level residualization commute by linearity)

### Infrastructure
- Embeddings: `synthesis/embeddings.npz` (BGE-M3, 384 yaoci × 1024 dim; also guaci, tuan, daxiang)
- Cross-model embeddings: `Q1/embeddings_{bge-m3,e5-large,labse,sikuroberta}.npz`
- Atlas: `atlas/atlas.json`
- Residual extraction: `Q1/phase1_residual_structure.py`
- GPU: Quadro P5000, 16GB. sentence-transformers + FlagEmbedding in venv.

### Reusable Methodological Patterns
- **R² granularity check:** Centroid-level R² absorbs within-unit variance. Always verify R² at the granularity matching the established measurement (384 lines, not 64 centroids).
- **Null findings under distorted residuals are conservative:** Noisy residuals bias correlations toward zero. R235 is robust precisely because it's null.
- **Partial correlation for mediation:** Simpler than formal mediation analysis (no causal model required). The < 1% reduction in R233 is the cleanest possible mediation null.
- **Random-baseline comparison for structural features:** Compare reversal group to R159 profile at matching Hamming distance, rather than constructing matched permutation nulls at small n.

### Research Program Dynamics
- R191 retraction demonstrates healthy methodology: proposed generalization → systematic test → failure → retraction.
- Authorial confound (tuan > guaci > yaoci pattern) recurs across multiple analyses. Layer-stratified signals require skepticism.
- The "numerically positive, structurally negative" distinction (T3) is reusable: cardinality match ≠ structural match.
- Cross-model validation standard: ρ > 0.78 for Tier 1b. Single-model p ≈ 0.08 does not meet bar.
- The distributed signature (no component significant, aggregate significant) in R236 is the same non-decomposability seen at every resolution. The manifold's resistance to factoring is its defining characteristic.
- Investigation convergence through non-decomposability: when the sole surprising finding (R236) decomposes into the same holistic structure seen everywhere else, the investigation has reached its natural boundary.
