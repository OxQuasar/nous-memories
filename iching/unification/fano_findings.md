## Synthesis

### The Three-Line Architecture

| Line | Functional | Pair | Type | Role |
|---|---|---|---|---|
| **H** | ker(b₁⊕b₂) | 震/巽 Wood/Wood | k₀ | H-subgroup, 互 kernel, divination |
| **Q** | ker(b₀⊕b₂) | 坎/離 Water/Fire | k₁ | Palindromic, bridge singletons |
| **P** | ker(b₀⊕b₁) | 兌/艮 Metal/Earth | k₂ | Parity separator, partition axis |

### Key Findings

1. **The Fano plane organizes the 五行 structure.** The three lines through
   OMI carry exactly the three complement pairs, classified into the three
   structurally distinct destination types (k₀, k₁, k₂).

2. **V₄ kernel = spaceprobe block preservers.** The exact sequence
   1 → V₄ → Stab(H) → S₃ → 1 has V₄ kernel that is precisely the
   subgroup preserving the spaceprobe's block system. The V₄ acts on
   coset 1 as S₂×S₂, swapping within {艮,兌} and {坎,離}. The S₃
   quotient permutes line H but breaks blocks. The full spaceprobe S₄
   requires non-linear maps not in GL(3,F₂).

3. **Within-block XORs live on line H.** Despite Stab(H) not preserving
   the spaceprobe blocks, the within-block difference masks {O, OMI}
   are all elements of H. The block structure is built FROM H-line
   elements, even though it is not preserved BY the H-stabilizer.

4. **P is forced as the parity axis.** Among the three lines through OMI,
   only P = ker(b₀⊕b₁) keeps all three doubleton pairs within cosets.
   The 五行 partition requires P; H and Q would break it.

5. **The 後天 derivation factors cleanly by prime.** After the non-linear
   Z₅ constraint (factor 12), the residual is Z₂³ with F₂-linear
   constraints from Z₂ and Z₃ (factor 2³ = 8). Total: 96 = 12 × 8.

### The Two S₄ Groups: Intersection and Divergence

Stab(H) and the spaceprobe's S₄ overlap at V₄ but diverge beyond it:

- **Stab(H)**: exact sequence 1 → V₄ → S₄ → S₃ → 1. The V₄ kernel
  preserves spaceprobe blocks; the S₃ quotient permutes line H.
- **Spaceprobe S₄**: faithful on 4 blocks, requires non-linear maps.

The V₄ is the maximal subgroup of Stab(H) that preserves the spaceprobe
block system. Whether V₄ is also contained in the spaceprobe's S₄
(as a subgroup of S₈) remains to be verified with the explicit spaceprobe
permutation group.

This means the H-line and the block system are connected but not
equivalent structures: the linear stabilizer of the line CONTAINS
the block-preserving involutions but also includes elements that
break the blocks by permuting the line.
