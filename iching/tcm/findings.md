## Domain criterion (updated)

Three necessary conditions for a QвӮғ domain to be testable against the ZвӮ… grammar:

1. **Three independent binary axes** вҖ” mutual information between any two axes вүҲ 0
2. **Commensurable axes** вҖ” all three measured the same way, same kind of quantity, similar timescales
3. **Matching axis-type hierarchy** вҖ” domain's axis asymmetry aligns with algebra's (pure-е…Ӣ axis matches domain's most adversarial axis)

**D4 (markets):** Failed #2 (trend вү  vol вү  liquidity) and #3 (no alignment).
**D1 (TCM):** Failed #1 (axes share йҷ°/йҷҪ factor) and additionally #3 (native structure is 2Г—3, not 2Ві).

The failures are complementary вҖ” markets had independent but incommensurable axes; TCM had commensurable but correlated axes. The criterion demands all three conditions simultaneously.
