# Probe 3: 用神 Projection — Where Meaning Enters

## A. The Querrent's Position on the 六親 Cycle

### The 六親 生 cycle

```
父母 →生→ 兄弟 →生→ 子孫 →生→ 妻財 →生→ 官鬼 →生→ 父母
```

### The triad for each 用神 type

| 用神 | Domains | Auxiliary (σ⁻¹) | 忌神 (what 克s 用) |
|------|---------|-----------------|------------------|
| 兄弟 | 0 | 父母 | 官鬼 |
| 子孫 | 4 | 兄弟 | 妻財 |
| 父母 | 3 | 官鬼 | 兄弟 |
| 妻財 | 7 | 子孫 | 父母 |
| 官鬼 | 8 | 妻財 | 子孫 |

### 兄弟 (self) on the 克 cycle

```
克 cycle: 官鬼 →克→ 兄弟 →克→ 父母 →克→ 妻財 →克→ 子孫 →克→ 官鬼
```

The querrent (兄弟) is:
- **克ed by 官鬼** (external authority constrains the self)
- **克s 父母** (self disrupts structure? — note: this is less intuitive)
- **克s 妻財** (self depletes resources — '破財之人')

### Why 兄弟 is never 用神

兄弟 has 0 domains. The text says: '破財之人，不為主、不為輔'
('The one who depletes wealth is neither master nor support').

The querrent (兄弟 = same element as palace) is the **reference frame**,
not the object of inquiry. You don't divine about yourself; you divine about
what acts on you (官鬼), what you seek (妻財), what you tend (子孫),
what shelters you (父母).

### Geometric claim: 兄弟 sits between the two heaviest 用神

On the 克 cycle: 官鬼 →克→ **兄弟** →克→ … →克→ 妻財

克 cycle order: 官鬼 → 兄弟 → 父母 → 妻財 → 子孫
- 官鬼 to 兄弟: 1 step(s) on 克 cycle
- 兄弟 to 妻財: 2 step(s) on 克 cycle
- 官鬼 (8 domains) + 妻財 (7 domains) = 15 of 22 total domains

**兄弟 is one 克-step from the heaviest node (官鬼, 8 domains) and two steps
from the second heaviest (妻財, 7 domains).** Together, 官 and 財 account for
15/22 = 68% of all domains.

## E. Shadow Hit Probability

The seasonal system assigns 5 strength levels to 5 elements.
In each season, exactly 1 element is 囚 and 1 is 死 — two 'suppressed' elements.
If the 用神's element happens to be 囚 or 死, the reading is temporally disadvantaged.

### Suppression rate by 用神 type

| 用神 type | Suppressed contexts | Total contexts | Rate |
|----------|--------------------:|:--------------:|------|
| 官鬼 | 16 | 40 | 40% |
| 妻財 | 16 | 40 | 40% |
| 子孫 | 16 | 40 | 40% |
| 父母 | 16 | 40 | 40% |

Each element is 囚 in one season and 死 in one season → suppressed in 2/5 seasons.
Since 六親→element is a bijection per palace, each 用神 type is suppressed in exactly
2/5 = 40% of season-contexts. The rate is **uniform across 用神 types and palaces.**

## Synthesis

### The asymmetry is in the question space, not the structural space

The 五行 cycle's symmetry ensures uniform temporal treatment: every 用神 type is
suppressed in exactly 2/5 of seasonal contexts, 克ed by 1/5 of daily elements.
The structural incompleteness (6/8 palaces lack 2 六親 types) is also symmetric
at the type level — each type is missing from roughly the same number of hexagrams.

The asymmetry enters through the 用神 mapping: 官鬼 covers 8 domains, 妻財 covers 7,
while 子孫 covers 4 and 父母 only 3. This means the *practical impact* of structural
blindness is concentrated: when 官鬼 is missing from a hexagram, 8 question domains
cannot be read; when 父母 is missing, only 3.

### The domain-weighted blindness reveals practical asymmetry

| 用神 | Missing rate | Domains | Impact = miss × domains |
|------|:-----------:|:-------:|:----------------------:|
| 官鬼 | 10/64 | 8 | 80 |
| 妻財 | 11/64 | 7 | 77 |
| 子孫 | 21/64 | 4 | 84 |
| 父母 | 9/64 | 3 | 27 |

官鬼's high domain count means its structural absences have disproportionate impact.
妻財 is close behind. Together, the two heaviest types (官鬼+妻財, 15/22 domains)
account for the majority of practical blindness.

### 兄弟's structural role: benign absence, information-theoretic gain

兄弟 has 0 domains — its absence from a hexagram costs nothing in question coverage.
When 兄弟 is one of the two missing types in a palace, the *effective* darkness is
determined entirely by the other missing type. This means the structural incompleteness
is less severe than it appears: about half the time, one of the two missing types is
the zero-weight 兄弟.

### The triad structure creates diagnostic gradients

Only a fraction of hexagram-用神 contexts provide full diagnostic information
(signal + support + threat all present). The remainder are degraded in specific ways:
Blind (can't read), Exposed (can read but vulnerable), Unguarded (can read but
can't see threats), Isolated (signal only, no context).
This gradient is the mechanism through which structure constrains meaning —
not by preventing readings entirely, but by controlling how much diagnostic
context is available for each question type.

### Connection to the central thread

The 用神 mapping is Layer 3 of the I Ching's interpretive system:
1. **Structure** (Z₂⁶ algebra, trigram decomposition) → the space
2. **Incompleteness** (missing 六親 types) → curvature of the space
3. **用神 projection** (question → 六親 type) → meaning enters the curved space

The key finding: the structural space is symmetric (五行 cycle treats all types
equally), but the 用神 projection is asymmetric (8 domains for 官鬼, 0 for 兄弟).
It is this projection — not the algebra — that creates the practical asymmetries
practitioners navigate. The question doesn't just enter a space; it enters a space
that is uniformly curved but non-uniformly illuminated by the question's own category.
