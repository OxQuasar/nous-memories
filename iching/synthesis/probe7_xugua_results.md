## 5. Interpretation

### Statistical summary

All tests below are on the 31 between-pair transitions only.

| Test | Statistic | p-value | Sig |
|------|-----------|---------|-----|
| Category × basin preservation | χ²=6.742 | 0.3454 |  |
| Category × depth direction | χ²=8.887 | 0.7125 |  |
| Category × I-component | χ²=7.890 | 0.2462 |  |
| Category × meeting-point 五行 | χ²=23.445 | 0.4937 |  |

**No significant associations** between 序卦 narrative categories and algebraic
transition properties. The reasoning the 序卦傳 uses to justify the King Wen
sequence does not correlate with basin, depth, I-component, or five-phase
relations between consecutive hexagrams.

### Reversal narratives

Of 12 reversal narratives across all 63 transitions, 7 are within-pair KW partners. These are the trivial within-pair transitions where reversal/complement
is the algebraic relationship by construction. The 5 between-pair
reversal narratives describe conceptual reversal ('things cannot remain X forever')
but this does NOT correspond to algebraic reversal/complement operations.

### Consequence narratives and 生

Consequence-logic narratives ('X必Y') do not show elevated 生 meeting-point relations compared to other categories.
The 序卦's causal reasoning does not systematically track the five-phase
生 (generation) relationship between consecutive hexagrams' trigrams.
