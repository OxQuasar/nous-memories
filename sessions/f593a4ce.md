```

  M = h₂ ⊕ h₃  (interface symmetry)
  I = h₃ ⊕ h₂  (same as M, by commutativity)
```

**M = I always in 互 space.** The overlapping trigram construction fuses middle and interface checks. H is the kernel algebra compatible with this fusion. The outer hexagram has full Z₂³, but 互 only has Z₂² (two independent symmetry checks, not three).
